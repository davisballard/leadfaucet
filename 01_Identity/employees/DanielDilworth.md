## Professional Bio

Daniel Dilworth is a product and UX designer based in Los Angeles with close to 15 years of experience in data-driven, user-based design practices. As Founder and CEO of Lead Faucet, Dan brings a unique blend of strategic thinking, visual design excellence, and behavioral design methodology to drive the company's vision, growth, and product development.

Throughout his career, Dan has partnered with industry-leading companies to help build and improve their core experiences. His client roster includes Amazon, eBay, PayPal, Intuit, Mazda, Kia, Toyota, Royal Caribbean, Adidas, Bosch, Fossil, Epson, Exact Sciences, and MindBody—spanning e-commerce, fintech, automotive, travel & hospitality, sports & lifestyle, healthcare, and legal technology sectors.

Dan's work encompasses the full spectrum of digital creative disciplines: product design, UX research, visual design, marketing strategy, social media marketing, and commercial photography. His approach combines user-centered methodology with business strategy, creating experiences that drive measurable outcomes while maintaining design excellence.
