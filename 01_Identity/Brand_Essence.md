# Brand Essence: Lead Faucet

## What We Are

Lead Faucet is a **lead generation company** specializing in **Motor Vehicle Accident (MVA) personal injury** for law firms. We are NOT a law firm — we are a lead aggregator.

### Practice Areas

Lead Faucet goes deep on three verticals — each with its own dedicated tools, screening criteria, and creative strategy. The system is vertical-agnostic. The expertise is not.

**Personal Injury (MVA)** — Car, motorcycle, truck/semi, rideshare (Uber/Lyft). Soft tissue through catastrophic. Pre-qualified claimants with verified injuries, exclusive to your market.

**Immigration** — Family petitions, employment visas, asylum, naturalization. Multi-language intake across 12 visa categories, powered by GetMyVisa.

**Mass Tort** — Emerging and active mass torts. High-volume qualification systems for complex, multi-district litigation — pharmaceutical, product liability, corporate harm.

We do NOT generate leads for:
- Workplace injuries
- Slip and fall
- Medical malpractice

### The Business Model
1. Lead Faucet runs its own ad accounts on Meta + Google
2. Ads are *generic* (not attorney-specific) → third-person language only
3. Leads are captured via landing page forms
4. Leads are sold/sent to partner law firms (Jacoby & Meyers, Think Law, etc.)

## The Unique Mechanism: "Case-Ready Leads"

**This is Lead Faucet's ownable phrase. No competitor can claim it.**

> *"We don't send you leads. We send you cases — pre-documented, pre-qualified, ready for intake. Most lead gen companies deliver a phone number. We deliver a CaseSnapshot PDF."*

**Case-Ready is a standard of delivery, not a case type.**

Lead Faucet delivers across the full case spectrum — soft tissue through catastrophic. Every lead, regardless of case type or source, is held to the Case-Ready standard: superior creative attracts real intent, tighter funnels screen out noise, and proprietary tools document cases before attorney contact begins. The attorney receives a case, not a contact.

**The proof points (always name both):**
- **CaseSnapshot** — Free AI-assisted tool for MVA victims. Case documented in five minutes: injury, liability, treatment. Professional PDF delivered with the lead.
- **GetMyVisa** — Multi-language guided intake for immigration applicants across 12 visa categories. Applicant pre-qualified and documented before attorney contact.

Both tools are upstream of everything else in the category. No other legal lead gen company builds consumer-facing documentation tools.

**Critical distinction:** CaseSnapshot and GetMyVisa are proof of the Case-Ready documentation standard — not indicators of case value. A soft-tissue lead generated through a quiz funnel is still Case-Ready when our ads attract real intent and our funnels screen out noise. The differentiator is how we deliver, not what case type it is.

**System-level phrase:** "The Case-Ready Pipeline"
