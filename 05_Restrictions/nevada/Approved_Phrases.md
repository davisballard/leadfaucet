## 3. SETTLEMENT LANGUAGE (Nevada — With Disclaimer)

### Past Results Mentions (Nevada-Approved)

> **Saul:** "California SB37 makes this dicey. Nevada? Green light, as long as you include the disclaimer."

**Approved Formats:**

**Aggregate Claims:**
- "Our attorneys have recovered over $500 million for accident victims."
- "Over $100 million in settlements for Nevada clients."
- "Billions recovered for injury victims nationwide."

**Case-Specific (Anonymized):**
- "Recent Nevada settlement: $1.2 million for rear-end collision victim."
- "Our client received $350,000 after being told their case was worth nothing."
- "Slip and fall verdict: $750,000 for Las Vegas client."

**Required Disclaimer (ALWAYS INCLUDE):**
```
Past results do not guarantee future outcomes. Each case is 
evaluated individually based on its specific facts.
```

### Comparison Claims (Nevada-Approved)

**"More Than First Offer" Framing:**
> ⚠️ California SB37 BANS this type of claim. Nevada allows it.

- "Insurance offered $5,000. We got $50,000."
- "They said the case was worth $10,000. Jury said $250,000."
- "Their offer: $2,500. Our result: $175,000."

**Always Follow With:**
> "Results vary based on individual case facts. Past results do not guarantee future outcomes."

| Copy Element | Nevada Version | California Version |
|--------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Hook** | "Vegas crash? Fight back." | "Involved in an accident? Know your options." |
| **Settlement** | "We recovered $1.2M for a client like you." | "Our attorneys have experience with accident cases." |
| **Urgency** | "Don't wait another day." | "Statutes of limitations may apply." |
| **CTA** | "Get your money." | "Learn about your legal options." |
| **Tone** | Fighter, aggressive, direct | Trusted advisor, educational, soft |
