## Critical Rules (Read First)

1. **"YOU" IS BANNED** ‚Äî Never use second person when referencing injury/pain/condition
2. **Third person only** ‚Äî "Accident victims may be entitled..." not "Are you injured?"
3. **We are NOT a law firm** ‚Äî We are a lead aggregator, disclose accordingly
4. **One-to-one consent** ‚Äî FCC 2025 requires named entities, no "marketing partners" lists
5. **Special Ad Categories** ‚Äî Limited targeting on Meta, no lookalikes on injury data
6. **FIRM NAMES ONLY IN 3 PLACES** ‚Äî Partner firm names (Larry H. Parker, Jacoby & Meyers) can ONLY appear in: (1) Form fine print, (2) Consent checkbox, (3) Thank You page header. Use their stats/testimonials in ads, but NOT the firm name.
7. **NO BLAND IMAGES** ‚Äî ALL images MUST follow `10_Ad_Factory/Production_Guidelines/Image_Style_Guide.md`. Nevada-specific, amateur iPhone quality, 3-5 easter eggs, match the ad angle. No stock-looking AI slop.
8. **VIDEO CLIP CONTINUITY** ‚Äî Same person must appear across all clips in a multi-clip video. If Clip 2 shows a man with a cast, Clip 3 must show the SAME man.
9. **SCRIPT TIMING** ‚Äî Target 1.5-2.1 words per second. 15-sec video = **23-31 words**. Music/ambiance continues ~2-3 sec after VO ends.
10. **AUDIO MIX** ‚Äî Voiceover 100%, Environment 50% (LOUD ‚Äî must be clearly audible), Music 30%. Music + environment run FULL video duration.
11. **VOICE** ‚Äî Use `Bill` (gruff, working-class American) for Nevada ads. Alternative: `Adam` (deep, mature).
